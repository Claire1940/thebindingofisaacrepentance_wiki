<Section icon={Shield} title="Repentance+ and Online Co-op Features">

In 2026, the **Repentance+** update has transformed the game into a social experience. The integrated online co-op system allows up to four players to tackle runs together with full Steam matchmaking support.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Install Repentance+">
    Ensure you have all previous DLCs (Rebirth, Afterbirth, Afterbirth+, and Repentance) installed on Steam. The Repentance+ update is a free addition for owners.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Lobby Setup">
    Choose between Friend Match, Public Match, or Quick Match from the main menu. The host can set the difficulty to Normal, Hard, or Greedier mode.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Character Selection">
    Each player selects a character from their own save file. Shared resources include coins, bombs, and keys, while health and items are individual.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Shared Progression">
    Defeating bosses online grants completion marks to all eligible players simultaneously, making it an efficient way to knock out difficult marks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The online system includes a built-in emote wheel and text chat, allowing for coordination during item choices or secret room hunting. This update alone has revitalized the community and made the game worth it for players who found solo runs too isolating.
