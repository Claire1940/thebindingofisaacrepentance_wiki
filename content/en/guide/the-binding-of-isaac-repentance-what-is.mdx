<Section icon={Target} title="Core Gameplay Mechanics and Stats">

Understanding the underlying systems is the first step to mastering the dungeon. Every run begins with Isaac (or another character) in the Basement. Your goal is to navigate a series of randomly generated rooms, each containing enemies, obstacles, or rewards. 

The "Found HUD" option is mandatory for serious players in 2026. It displays your numerical stats on the left side of the screen, allowing you to see exactly how an item affects your performance.

| Stat | Icon | Description | Critical Threshold |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Essential Items and S-Tier Synergies">

Items are the lifeblood of a run. While there are hundreds of options, a few stand out as "run-winners" that can carry you to the final boss regardless of your other gear.

**Top Tier Items for 2026 Runs:**
- **Sacred Heart**: Massive damage multiplier + Homing tears.
- **C Section**: Fetus projectiles that deal massive ticking damage.
- **Glitched Crown**: Allows you to cycle through items on a pedestal to pick the best one.
