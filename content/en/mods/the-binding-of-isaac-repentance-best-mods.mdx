These mods address these frustrations while keeping the core combat and difficulty intact.

The standout in this category is **GoodTrip [Fixed]**. This mod allows you to click on any previously cleared room on the minimap to teleport there instantly. This is particularly useful for returning to a Shop, a Sacrifice Room, or a forgotten pickup after defeating a floor boss. It does not allow teleporting into uncleared rooms, ensuring that the challenge of exploration remains preserved.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Music and Aesthetic Customization">

The atmosphere of the game is iconic, but after thousands of runs, the soundtrack can become repetitive. The modding community has created robust frameworks that allow you to swap the music for the original Flash Isaac tracks, the Antibirth soundtrack, or even completely custom music packs.

To manage these changes, **Soundtrack Menu** is an essential utility. It provides an in-game interface to switch between installed music packs on the fly. This avoids the need to manually enable or disable mods in the main menu. Most high-quality music mods, such as **Antibirth Music+++**, require a framework like **Music Mod Callback** to function correctly, ensuring that the music transitions smoothly between rooms and boss phases.

| Framework | Best For | Requirement |
