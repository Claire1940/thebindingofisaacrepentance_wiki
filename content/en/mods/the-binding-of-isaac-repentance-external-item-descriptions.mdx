<Section icon={Settings} title="Installing the Binding of Isaac Repentance External Item Descriptions Mod">

Installing the **the binding of isaac repentance external item descriptions** (EID) mod is a straightforward process through the Steam Workshop. This mod functions as a real-time overlay, reading the game's internal memory to provide accurate data without disabling achievements (provided you have defeated Mom at least once). For players aiming for the Dead God achievement, EID is indispensable for identifying which items contribute to specific transformations or have dangerous anti-synergies.

<Callout type="success" title="Achievement Safety">
In Repentance, mods do not disable achievements or completion marks as long as you have defeated the Mom boss in the Depths at least once on that save file.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Info} title="Core Features and Display Mechanics">

The primary function of the **the binding of isaac repentance external item descriptions** mod is to eliminate the need for external wiki searches. When Isaac stands near a pedestal item, a text box appears on the left side of the screen. This box contains the item's name, its quality (ranked 0 to 4), and a detailed breakdown of its mechanical effects.

<Callout type="tip" title="Quality Matters">
Items are ranked from Quality 0 (weak/situational) to Quality 4 (run-defining). EID displays this number prominently, helping you decide whether to take an item or use a reroll tool like the D6.
</Callout>

Beyond simple pedestals, EID tracks several other critical gameplay elements:
- **Trinkets:** Displays the passive effect of the held or floor-bound trinket.
- **Cards and Runes:** Explains exactly what a card does before you use it, including reversed tarot cards.
- **Pills:** Once a pill is identified, EID shows its effect. It can also show effects for unidentified pills if you have the "PHD" or "False PHD" items.
- **Transformations:** Shows a small icon indicating if an item contributes to transformations like Guppy, Beelzebub, or Conjoined.

| Display Element | Description | Visual Indicator |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Item Name** | The official name of the collectible | Bold Header |
| **Effect Text** | Stat changes (Damage, Tears, etc.) | Bulleted List |
| **Quality** | Power level from 0 to 4 | Colored Number |
| **Item Pool** | Where the item originated (Angel, Devil, etc.) | Text Label |
| **Mod Source** | Indicates if the item is from a mod | Subtext |

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Advanced Functionality for Tainted Characters">

For advanced players, the **the binding of isaac repentance external item descriptions** mod provides specialized tools for complex characters. Tainted Cain, for example, relies entirely on the Bag of Crafting. EID reads the pickups (coins, bombs, keys, hearts) currently on the floor of the room and generates a list of possible items you can craft.

<Callout type="warning" title="Bag of Crafting Recipes">
In the current version of Repentance, many Bag of Crafting recipes are randomized based on your run's seed. EID calculates these locally, ensuring the recipes displayed are accurate for your specific game.
</Callout>

Another powerful interaction is with the **Spindown Dice**. When you hold the Spindown Dice, EID will display what the current pedestal item will turn into after one use. This is vital for targeting high-value items like Death Certificate or Sacred Heart by "spinning down" lower-tier items with specific ID numbers.
