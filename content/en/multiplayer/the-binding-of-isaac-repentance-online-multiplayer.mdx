  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to Betas">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enter Beta Code">

  <Step num="4" title="Select the Branch">

  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Launch the Game">

  </Step>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Mechanics & Rules">

Understanding the underlying rules of **the binding of isaac repentance online multiplayer** is essential for high-level play. The game handles death and resurrection differently than the base game to prevent "game over" screens from a single player's mistake.

When a player dies, they turn into a ghost. As a ghost, they can still provide minor assistance by dealing very low damage and blocking certain projectiles. To respawn, the surviving players must either clear the current floor or find a specific revival item.

**Key Multiplayer Rules:**
- **Shared Camera**: The camera attempts to keep all players in view. If a player lags behind, they will be "dragged" toward the group.
- **Health Management**: Red hearts are individual, but certain "All Stats Up" items may provide minor buffs to the whole team.
- **Achievements**: You can unlock characters like *The Forgotten* or *Bethany* in multiplayer, provided the host has the requirements met.
