Success in **the binding of isaac repentance local coop** hinges on resource distribution. Because the game does not scale the number of item drops based on the number of players, a four-player team will have the same number of items as a solo player. To compensate, players must utilize character-specific strengths. For example, giving all HP Up items to Magdalene allows her to act as a tank for the team, while giving damage multipliers to Azazel ensures the team has enough DPS to clear bosses quickly.
