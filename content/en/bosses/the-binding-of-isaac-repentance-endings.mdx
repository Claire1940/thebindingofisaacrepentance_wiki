<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock the Final Ending (The Beast)">

Reaching the final ending in Repentance is a multi-step process that begins in Depth II. Unlike standard runs, this path requires you to "backtrack" through the floors you have already cleared. This sequence is known as "The Ascent."

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Defeat Mother Once">
    Before you can access the final ending, you must have defeated Mother at least once on your save file to unlock the Strange Door in Depths II.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Retrieve the Polaroid or Negative">
    Proceed to Depths II and defeat Mom. Take either the Polaroid or the Negative. You will need a way to teleport out of the boss room, such as a Joker card, a generic teleport pill, or by using the Fool card found in a tinted skull on the floor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Open the Strange Door">
    Return to the starting room of Depths II and use the Polaroid or Negative on the Strange Door. This leads to the Mausoleum/Gehenna II boss room, where you must defeat Mom's Heart.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Ascent">
    Enter the beam of light to begin the Ascent. You will travel backward through every floor you visited, encountering "tainted" versions of standard enemies. Reach the first floor to find the trapdoor to Home.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Final Confrontation">
    In the Home chapter, sleep in Isaac's bed, watch the television, and prepare for the battle against Dogma and subsequently The Beast and the Four Horsemen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Inventory Management">
During the Ascent, you cannot find new item rooms. Ensure your build is powerful enough to handle the Four Horsemen and The Beast before you leave Depths II.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="The Mother Ending: The Alternate Path">

The Mother ending is often considered the most difficult mechanical challenge in the game. To reach Mother, players must navigate the alternate floor sets and assemble the "Knife." This path is significantly more dangerous than the standard route due to the increased health and complex patterns of alternate enemies.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Knife Piece 1">
    Found in Downpour/Dross II. You must touch a white fireplace to become a lost soul, then enter the mirror to find the piece in the treasure room.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Knife Piece 2">
    Found in Mines/Ashpit II. You must activate three yellow switches and ride a minecart into a dark area, escaping a shadow entity.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

Defeating Mother unlocks the "Strange Door," which is the gatekeeper for the final ending. The fight itself consists of two grueling phases where Mother uses her knife and projectiles to corner the player.

| Phase | Health % | Primary Attacks | Recommended Items |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1** | 100% - 50% | Knife throws, Fist slams | High speed, Psy Fly, Shielding tears |
| **Phase 2** | 50% - 0% | Spin attacks, Grid projectiles | High DPS, Stopwatch, I'm Drowsy pill |

<Callout type="success" title="Advanced Tactic">
Using the "I'm Drowsy" pill during the second phase of the Mother fight makes her bullet patterns significantly easier to weave through, as it slows down all projectiles.
</Callout>
