This path is significantly more difficult than the standard route, featuring enemies with higher health and more aggressive projectiles.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter the Secret Exit">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Mirror World">
    In Downpour II, find the white fire and walk into it to become a lost-form character. Enter the mirror to find the first Knife Piece in the Treasure Room.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Abandoned Mineshaft">
    Enter Mines or Ashpit II using two bombs. Press three yellow buttons hidden throughout the floor, then enter the mineshaft to escape the Great Gideon's shadow with the second Knife Piece.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Flesh Door">
    In Mausoleum II, defeat the alternate version of Mom. Use the completed Knife to stab the red flesh door, leading to a fight with the alternate Mom's Heart.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Superbosses: Hush and Delirium">

Hush and Delirium are optional encounters that provide some of the most powerful unlocks in the game. Hush is a timed encounter, requiring you to defeat Mom's Heart within 30 minutes. Delirium is found in The Void, a floor that has a chance to appear after defeating any major endgame boss.

| Superboss | Health (HP) | Armor Mechanic | Best Build Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hush** | 6,666 | Extreme Scaling | High Fire Rate / Shielding |
| **Delirium** | 10,000 | Variable Scaling | Homing / High Movement Speed |

**Hush Strategy Highlights:**
- **Continuum Tears**: These tears wrap around the screen. Find a "safe spot" where the pattern doesn't overlap and stand still.
- **Hush Flies**: Clear these immediately as they can overwhelm your positioning.
- **Blue Womb Prep**: Use the four chests and two item rooms in the Blue Womb to finalize your build.

**Delirium Strategy Highlights:**
- **Form Shifting**: Delirium transforms into bosses you've already defeated. It inherits their patterns but often moves much faster.
- **Telefragging**: Delirium is notorious for teleporting directly onto the player. High movement speed is essential to avoid these instances.
- **The Void**: Explore the entire floor before entering the boss room. There are multiple boss rooms, but only one contains Delirium.

<Callout type="success" title="Completion Marks">
Defeating Hush and Delirium on Hard Mode provides unique "blood-stained" marks on your character's post-it note. These are required for the "Dead God" achievement and unlock some of the best items in the game, such as the D-Infinity.
</Callout>
