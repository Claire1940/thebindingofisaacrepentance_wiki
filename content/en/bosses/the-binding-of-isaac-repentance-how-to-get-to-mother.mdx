Reaching the final boss of the Repentance expansion requires more than just skill; it requires a specific set of permanent unlocks. In **the binding of isaac repentance how to get to mother**, your journey begins long before you enter the first floor. The game gates this content behind the "A Secret Exit" achievement, which only triggers after you have successfully defeated Hush three times on any character.

Unlike standard floors, these alternate versions feature unique enemies, harder room layouts, and a specific questline involving the construction of a sacrificial knife.

<Section icon={Target} title="Constructing the Knife: Piece One">

The first step in your quest is obtaining **Knife Piece 1**. This occurs in **Downpour II** or **Dross II**. You must locate a white fireplace and a mirror within the same floor. Touching the white fire turns Isaac into a Lost-form character, allowing him to pass through the mirror into a mirrored version of the floor.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Find the White Fire">
    Locate the white fireplace in Downpour II. It does not deal damage but will transform you into The Lost.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter the Mirror">
    Walk through the mirror while in Lost-form. Be careful, as you will die in one hit while in this state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Retrieve the Piece">
    Navigate to the Treasure Room in the mirror world. The pedestal will contain Knife Piece 1.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Return Safely">
    Exit the Treasure Room and go back through the mirror to regain your original form and health.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Holy Mantle Protection">
If you have unlocked the Holy Mantle for The Lost (via the Greed Machine), it will apply to your transformed state in the mirror world, providing one free hit of protection.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Mausoleum and the Flesh Door">

With both pieces collected, they will combine into a powerful familiar that acts as a projectile. Your next destination is **Mausoleum I** or **Gehenna I**. To enter, you must pay two full hearts of health at the door found in the Mines II boss room. This floor is the final staging ground before the Corpse.

In **Mausoleum II**, you will face an enhanced version of the Mom boss fight. Once she is defeated, a strange, pulsating "Flesh Door" will appear in the boss room. This is where your constructed knife becomes essential.

<Callout type="warning" title="Door Cost">
The Mausoleum door takes two full hearts of damage. If you are playing a character like **The Lost** or **Tainted Judas**, ensure you have enough Soul Hearts or invulnerability frames to survive the transition.
</Callout>

**Mausoleum Floor Hazards:**
- **Teleporting Enemies**: Many foes in this area can bypass your line of sight.
- **Trap Tiles**: Watch for spikes and moving blades that deal full heart damage.
- **Enhanced Bosses**: Expect champion versions of standard bosses with higher health pools.
