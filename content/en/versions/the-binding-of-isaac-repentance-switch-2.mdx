<Section icon={Map} title="Getting Started: Core Loop & Setup Steps">

Succeeding in this challenging roguelike requires a balance of mechanical skill, resource management, and strategic decision-making. Every run consists of randomly generated floors where you must clear combat rooms, gather resources (coins, keys, and bombs), and acquire item upgrades to defeat the floor boss and descend deeper.

To maximize your chances of winning from your very first run on the new system, follow this optimized setup and progression sequence.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Optimize In-Game Settings">
    Before starting a run, navigate to the options menu. Turn on **Found HUD** to display your exact numerical stats (such as damage, fire rate, and luck) on the left side of the screen. Enable **Extra HUD** to display your collected items on the right side. Finally, set **Bullet Visibility** to high to make enemy projectiles stand out clearly against the dark floor textures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Health and Deal Mechanics">
    Understand the difference between Red Hearts, Soul Hearts, and Black Hearts. Taking damage to your Red Heart containers reduces your chances of spawning a Devil Deal or Angel Room after a boss fight. Always prioritize collecting Soul Hearts and Black Hearts, as they act as a defensive shield that preserves your deal-room spawn percentages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear Rooms in the Correct Order">
    When entering a new floor, do not rush to the boss room. Clear the standard combat rooms first to accumulate coins, keys, and bombs. Locate the **Treasure Room** (which contains a free item) and the **Shop** (where you can purchase health, keys, and utility items). Only enter the Boss Room once you have fully explored the floor and spent your resources wisely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Choose Your Deal Path">
    After defeating a floor boss from Floor 2 onward, a Devil Deal or Angel Room may appear. In this game version, skipping the first Devil Deal entirely guarantees that the next deal room to spawn will be an **Angel Room**. Angel Rooms offer powerful offensive and defensive items completely free of charge, making them highly recommended for consistent runs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Resource Tip">
Always keep at least one key in reserve for the next floor's Treasure Room. Entering a floor with zero keys can lock you out of a vital item upgrade, severely hindering your offensive scaling.
