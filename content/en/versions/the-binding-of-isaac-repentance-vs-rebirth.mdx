<Section icon={Target} title="Item Balance, Synergies, and Meta Shifts">

The transition from Rebirth to Repentance completely overhauled the item sandbox. In Rebirth, a few highly dominant items like Brimstone, Mom's Knife, and Godhead could easily carry a run to victory with minimal support. Repentance introduced extensive balance changes, reducing the raw power of these outlier items while drastically expanding the synergy system.

In Repentance, items interact with each other in logical, visual, and mechanical ways. For example, lasers, bombs, and tears now combine dynamically rather than overriding one another, making average items viable when combined correctly.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Multiplayer and Online Co-op Overhaul">

One of the most requested features throughout the history of the franchise was a robust multiplayer mode. In Rebirth, co-op was limited to a local-only system where secondary players joined as dependent, fragile babies that drained health from Player 1's pool. This system made true cooperative play difficult and unrewarding.

Repentance+ completely replaces this system with integrated online co-op for up to four players, featuring shared progression, native matchmaking, and independent character selection.

| Feature | Rebirth Co-op | Repentance+ Online Co-op |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Player Limit** | Up to 4 players | Up to 4 players |
| **Character Choice** | Player 2 plays as a dependent baby | Every player chooses a full character |
| **Connection Type** | Local / Steam Remote Play | Native Steam Matchmaking (Friend/Public) |
| **Progression** | Only host earns achievements | All eligible players earn completion marks |
| **Resource Sharing** | Red hearts are subtracted from Player 1 | Coins, bombs, and keys are shared globally |

<Callout type="warning" title="Mod Compatibility Online">
