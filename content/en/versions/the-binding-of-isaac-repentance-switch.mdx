<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Unlock Roadmap">

Progression in **the binding of isaac repentance switch** is non-linear. To reach the "Dead God" achievement, you must follow a specific sequence of victories to unlock the true endings.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Mom Cycle">
    Defeat Mom in the Depths to unlock the Womb. You must defeat Mom's Heart 11 times to unlock **It Lives!**, which opens the permanent paths to the Cathedral and Sheol.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Polaroid & Negative">
    Defeat Isaac (Cathedral) 5 times to unlock **The Polaroid**. Defeat Satan (Sheol) 5 times to unlock **The Negative**. These items are required to enter the Chest and Dark Room respectively.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Secret Exit">
    Defeat **Hush** 3 times. This unlocks the Alternate Path (Downpour, Mines, Mausoleum). This path is significantly harder but necessary to reach the Mother boss.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Ascent">
    After defeating Mother once, you can find a "Strange Door" in Depths II. Use a teleport (The Fool card) after the Mom fight to open it and begin the Ascent toward the **Home** floor and **The Beast**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Completion Marks">
Every major boss yields a "Completion Mark" on a character's post-it note. Hard Mode is recommended because it grants both the Normal and Hard versions of the unlock simultaneously.
</Callout>
