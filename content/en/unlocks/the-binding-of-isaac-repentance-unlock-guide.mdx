<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Alternate Path & Ascent Guide">

To reach the final endings added in the Repentance DLC, you must navigate the complex Alternate Path to defeat Mother, which subsequently unlocks the Ascent route to Home and The Beast.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock the Alternate Path">
    Defeat Hush 3 times in the Blue Womb to open A Secret Exit. This allows alternate doors to spawn at the end of starting floors. Entering Downpour costs 1 key, Mines costs 2 bombs, and Mausoleum costs 2 full hearts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assemble the Knife">
    In Downpour II, touch the white fire to turn into the Lost, enter the mirror, and grab Knife Piece 1. In Mines II, find and press 3 yellow buttons, ride the minecart, and escape the shadow beast with Knife Piece 2.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat Mother">
    Use the completed knife on the flesh door in Mausoleum II after the alternate Mom fight. Defeat the alternate Mom's Heart, descend into the Corpse, and defeat Mother to unlock the Strange Door.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Ascent to Home">
    Start a new run, reach Depths II, and bomb the marked skull for a guaranteed Fool card. Defeat Mom, grab the Polaroid or Negative, teleport out, and open the Strange Door. Complete the reverse Ascent floors to reach Home.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Unlock Tainted Characters">
    Before starting the Ascent, drop any trinket in a Treasure or Boss Room. During the Ascent, return to that room to find it transformed into a Cracked Key. Use it on the hidden red closet in the hallway of Home.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success: The Ultimate Ending">
Sleeping in Mom's bed at Home and approaching the television triggers the Dogma boss fight, which leads directly into the final battle against the Ultra Horsemen and The Beast.
</Callout>
