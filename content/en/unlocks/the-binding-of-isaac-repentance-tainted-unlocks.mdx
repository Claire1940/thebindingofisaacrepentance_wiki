<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Unlocking Tainted Characters">

Unlocking Tainted characters requires a highly specific sequence of actions during a standard run. You must bypass the traditional ending paths and prepare a gateway tool before descending into the depths. Follow this precise routing guide to secure your first Tainted variant.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Drop a Trinket in a Boss or Treasure Room">
    During your descent through the main floors (Basement to Depths I), find any standard trinket. Drop this trinket directly inside a Treasure Room or a Boss Room. When you return to this room during the Ascent phase later, the trinket will have transformed into a **Cracked Key**, which is required to open the hidden closet in Home.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Marked Skull in Depths II">
    On Depths II, search the floor for a tinted skull marked with a small, faint cross. Bombing this skull is guaranteed to drop **The Fool** tarot card. Do not use this card immediately; save it for after the main boss fight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat Mom and Teleport Out">
    Enter the boss room on Depths II and defeat Mom. Pick up either **The Polaroid** or **The Negative** from the pedestal. Immediately use **The Fool** card to teleport out of the boss room, returning you to the starting room of Depths II.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Open the Strange Door">
    Approach the large, ominous door with a square slot in the starting room of Depths II. Touch the door while holding either The Polaroid or The Negative. The door will consume the photograph and swing open, granting access to the Mausoleum / Gehenna II alternate floor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Ascend to Home and Unlock the Character">
    Navigate through Mausoleum II, defeat the alternate boss, and grab **Dad's Note**. This initiates the Ascent. Run backward through all previous floors until you reach Home. Sleep in Mom's bed to open the hallway, walk to the living room, and use your **Cracked Key** (or the Red Key if found in Mom's Chest) on the red outline on the hallway wall to reveal the Tainted character closet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Tip">
You only need to drop a single trinket to get a Cracked Key. Leaving it in the very first Boss Room on Basement I is highly recommended, as you are guaranteed to pass back through it during the Ascent.
</Callout>
