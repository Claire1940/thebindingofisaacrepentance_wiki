<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Understanding Trinket Mechanics in Repentance">

Trinkets serve as vital passive pocket items that can completely redirect the trajectory of your run. Unlike standard passive items, you are normally restricted to holding just one of **the binding of isaac repentance trinkets** at a time. However, the Repentance expansion introduces several unique ways to bypass this limitation, upgrade existing trinket values, and even permanently absorb their effects into your character's passive pool.

In 2026, mastering the meta requires a deep understanding of Golden Trinkets and absorption mechanics. Golden Trinkets are rare variants that double the numerical effects of a standard trinket. If you find a way to stack these with multiplier items, your stat gains can quickly reach game-breaking levels.

| Mechanic | Default Limit | Upgrade Tool | Upgraded Limit |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Trinket Slots** | 1 Slot | Mom's Purse / Belly Button | 2 Slots |
| **Trinket Strength** | Baseline (1x) | Mom's Box / Golden Variant | 2x or 3x Strength |
| **Permanent Absorption** | None | Smelter / Gulp! Pill / Marbles | Infinite Passive Slots |

<Callout type="info" title="Mom's Box Interaction">
Holding Mom's Box grants a passive +1 Luck and doubles the effectiveness of any held trinket. If you hold a Golden Trinket while carrying Mom's Box, the effect is tripled (3x), turning minor stat boosts into run-defining upgrades.

<Section icon={Map} title="How to Break Trinket Limits: Steps to Smelt and Gulp">

To truly break your runs, you must bypass the standard carrying limits. By permanently absorbing trinkets, you keep their passive benefits active for the rest of the run without occupying your active trinket slots.

Follow these steps to successfully smelt or gulp your held trinkets into permanent passive buffs.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire an Absorption Tool">
    Locate the **Smelter** active item in a Shop, find a **Gulp!** pill via standard pill rotation, or unlock and pick up the **Marbles** passive item.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Drop or Locate the Desired Trinket">
    Hold your drop input button (default RT on controllers or Left Ctrl on keyboard) to drop your current trinket in a safe, cleared room. Ensure you are holding the exact trinket you want to absorb.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate the Absorption Effect">
    Use the Smelter while holding the trinket, consume the Gulp! pill, or take damage while holding the trinket if you have Marbles active.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Stack Multiple Permanent Passive Buffs">
    Repeat this process throughout the run. There is no limit to how many trinkets you can permanently absorb, allowing you to carry dozens of passive buffs simultaneously.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Persistent Absorption">
Permanently absorbed trinket effects persist even if you use the Clicker to swap characters, restart the run's floor layout via the R Key, or transition into the final Beast boss fight.
</Callout>
