<Section icon={Target} title="C & D Tiers: High-Risk Modifiers">

These tiers contain items that are either highly situational or carry significant risks. An item like **Soy Milk** can be a run-winner with **Lump of Coal** or **Rock Bottom**, but on its own, it can make it impossible to knock back enemies, leading to a quick death. D-Tier items are generally those you should reroll unless you have a specific synergy already in place.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Evaluate Your Synergies">
    Before picking up a C-Tier item like **Ipecac** or **Dr. Fetus**, check if you have explosion immunity (Pyromaniac or Host Hat). Without it, these items are just as likely to kill you as the enemies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check Your Hitbox">
    Items like **Guillotine** or **Isaac's Heart** change how you dodge. If you aren't comfortable with your hitbox being separated from your head or body, leave these items on the pedestal.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Assess the Chaos">
    **TMTRAINER** and **Missing No.** provide total randomness. Only take these if your current run is already failing and you need a "Hail Mary" to survive.
  </Step>
</Steps>
